I'm Asuai Chukwunalu JohnPaul, a graduate student and teaching assistant at North Dakota State University, Fargo. I study Electrical and Computer Engineering, with a focus on computer architecture, hardware security, and formal verification.

I have over five years of experience in cybersecurity, working in roles like cybersecurity engineer at Barclays UK and cybersecurity analyst at Zenith Bank. I’ve also taught students and helped them with projects in digital design and VHDL programming.

My goal is to build better and more secure computer systems. I’m passionate about using my skills to solve real-world security problems and help people learn about technology.
