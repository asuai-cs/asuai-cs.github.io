I’m Chukwunalu J. Asuai, a graduate student and teaching assistant at North Dakota State University, pursuing a Master’s in Electrical and Computer Engineering. My research focuses on high-performance computer architecture, hardware security, and formal verification, aiming to develop secure and efficient computing systems. With over five years of cybersecurity experience at organizations like Barclays UK and Zenith Bank, I specialize in threat detection, incident response, and security architecture. Previously, as a graduate assistant at the University of Uyo, Nigeria, I mentored students in digital design and VHDL programming. I am passionate about advancing cybersecurity through innovative research and aspire to contribute to academia via doctoral studies.
